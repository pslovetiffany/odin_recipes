Chicken soup Recipes

This is a description of the image above. The image above represents chicken soup in all its glory.

The chicken soup contains a nice steamy broth as well as chicken, carrots, celeries and of course, noodles. This dish is perfect for a cold winter day or if you are feeling under the weather. To make this recipe at home is a staple. Please enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 12 carrots, peeled and diced
  • 3 gloves garlic, minced
  • 8 cups chicken stock
  • 2 bay leaves (optional)

Steps to prepare the dish

  1. Melt butter in a large stockpot or Dutch oven over medium heat. Add onion, carrots and celery. Cook, stirring occasionally, until tender, about 3-4 minutes. Stir in garlic until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  2. hisk in chicken stock and bay leaves; season with salt and pepper, to taste.
  3. Stir in chicken and pasta and cook until tender, about 6-7 minutes.

Kimchi Jigae Recipe

This is a description of the image above. The image above represents kimchi jigae in all its glory.

Kimchi jigae is a staple korean dish. It is a delicious stew which is hot and great for a cold day. It usually contains long fermented kimchi, tofu, some sort of meat (i like spam), and a great broth.

Ingredients

  • 8 pounds of kimchi
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 12 slices of tofu
  • 3 gloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups anchovy stock
  • 2 cans of spam (optional)

Steps to prepare the dish

  1. Slice kimchi and fry in the pan with garlic and onions.
  2. Add kimchi to anchovy stock.
  3. Stir in spam and tofu and cook until tender, about 6-7 minutes.
